Case Study

Real-Time Telemetry Analytics

01Overview

Vehicle sensor data from a commercial fleet was being stored but rarely analyzed in time to prevent failures. The goal was to build a real-time analytics layer that could surface anomalies and trigger maintenance alerts before breakdowns occurred.

02Problem

Terabytes of raw telemetry data were archived daily but queried only in retrospective audits after incidents. Predictive maintenance was impossible without a streaming layer, leading to costly unplanned downtime averaging 14 hours per incident.

03Solution

A streaming analytics pipeline was deployed to process vehicle telemetry in real time. Machine learning models were trained on historical failure patterns and deployed at the edge to score each vehicle's health continuously. Alerts were routed to maintenance teams via a mobile dashboard.

04Results

83%

Unplanned downtime reduced

3 weeks

Avg. failure prediction lead time

4.7×

Maintenance ROI

Fleet operators now intervene proactively before failures occur. The reduction in unplanned downtime directly improved on-time delivery rates and customer satisfaction scores.
