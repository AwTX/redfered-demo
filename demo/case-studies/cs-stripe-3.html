Case Study

Global Marketplace Payouts

01Overview

A freelance marketplace needed to scale its seller payout infrastructure from 5 to 40 supported countries, handling multi-currency disbursements, local compliance requirements, and tax reporting obligations across jurisdictions with vastly different banking systems.

02Problem

International sellers waited up to 14 days for payouts via wire transfer, with high fees eating into earnings. Currency conversion was opaque, and compliance documentation for each new country had to be implemented from scratch.

03Solution

A unified payout orchestration layer was built to route payments through the optimal rails for each country — local ACH equivalents where available, instant payment schemes where supported. Currency conversion rates were locked at transaction time with transparent fee disclosure. A compliance framework was implemented to handle local KYC and tax documentation requirements.

04Results

40

Countries supported

1–2 days

Avg. payout time (down from 14)

65%

Payout fee reduction

Sellers in new markets report dramatically improved trust in the platform due to fast, transparent payouts. The payout infrastructure is now a competitive differentiator in seller acquisition.
