Case Study

Hybrid Cloud Migration

01Overview

A financial services firm needed to migrate hundreds of on-premises workloads to a hybrid cloud environment while maintaining strict regulatory compliance, zero downtime, and data residency requirements across multiple jurisdictions.

02Problem

The existing data center was approaching end-of-life, with hardware refresh costs projected at $40M. Legacy workloads had undocumented dependencies, and the compliance team required detailed audit trails for every data movement across borders.

03Solution

A phased migration strategy was designed: workloads were catalogued and dependency-mapped, then grouped into migration waves by risk level. Low-risk stateless services moved first, while stateful databases were replicated live before cutover. A compliance automation layer generated audit reports automatically throughout the process.

04Results

412

Workloads migrated

0

Compliance violations

$28M

5-year cost savings

The migration was completed six weeks ahead of schedule with no production incidents. The hybrid architecture gives the firm flexibility to burst to cloud for seasonal peaks while keeping sensitive data on-premises.
