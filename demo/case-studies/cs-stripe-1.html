Case Study

Payment Conversion Optimization

01Overview

A subscription SaaS company was losing a significant portion of potential customers at the payment step due to a cumbersome multi-page checkout flow, limited payment method support, and poor mobile UX. The goal was to redesign the payment experience to minimise drop-off.

02Problem

The checkout flow required 7 form fields and 3 page transitions, with no support for digital wallets or local payment methods. Mobile conversion rates were 40% lower than desktop, and error messages were cryptic and unhelpful.

03Solution

The checkout was rebuilt as a single-page experience with smart field autofill, real-time card validation, and support for 12 local payment methods including digital wallets. Error messages were redesigned with actionable guidance. Link-based checkout was introduced for returning customers.

04Results

22%

Overall conversion lift

38%

Mobile conversion improvement

7→1

Form fields at checkout

The redesigned checkout is the highest-converting in the company's history. The addition of local payment methods opened new markets in Southeast Asia and Latin America.
