Case Study

Data Sharing Monetization

01Overview

A weather data provider sought to monetize its proprietary datasets by enabling B2B customers to access live data directly in their own analytics environments, without the complexity of traditional data delivery via SFTP or APIs.

02Problem

Existing data delivery via API required customers to build and maintain ingestion pipelines, limiting the addressable market to technically sophisticated buyers. Revenue per customer was capped by API call pricing, which didn't reflect the full value customers derived from the data.

03Solution

A data sharing architecture was implemented that allowed customers to access live datasets directly within their own cloud accounts with zero-copy data transfer — no pipelines required. Usage-based pricing was introduced, with metering at the query level to capture value proportional to consumption.

04Results

3×

Revenue per customer uplift

180%

YoY new customer growth

0

Customer-side ingestion pipelines needed

The data marketplace has opened an entirely new customer segment of business analysts who previously lacked the technical capability to consume the provider's data. It has become the primary growth driver for the business.
