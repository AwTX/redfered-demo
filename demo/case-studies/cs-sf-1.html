Case Study

CRM Data Quality Overhaul

01Overview

A B2B enterprise had accumulated years of duplicated, incomplete, and outdated records in its CRM, undermining sales team confidence in the data and making marketing segmentation unreliable. The project aimed to establish a trusted, enriched customer database as the foundation for growth.

02Problem

An estimated 34% of CRM records contained duplicate entries, and 41% were missing critical firmographic data. Sales reps spent an average of 45 minutes per day manually verifying contact information before outreach.

03Solution

An automated data quality pipeline was built to deduplicate records using probabilistic matching, enrich missing fields via third-party data providers, and flag stale contacts for review. Ongoing data health monitoring was implemented with automated alerts when quality scores dropped below threshold.

04Results

34%

Duplicate records eliminated

45 min

Daily time saved per rep

28%

Email deliverability improvement

Sales teams now operate from a trusted data foundation, improving outreach effectiveness and pipeline predictability. Marketing campaigns benefit from reliable segmentation, improving ROI on demand generation spend.
