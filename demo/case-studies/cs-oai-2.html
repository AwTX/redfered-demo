Case Study · Partner 12

Conversational Customer Support

01Overview

A SaaS company's customer support team was overwhelmed by repetitive tier-1 queries that represented 70% of ticket volume but required relatively little expertise to resolve. The goal was to deploy an AI support assistant that could handle these queries end-to-end.

02Problem

Support agents spent the majority of their time on routine queries such as password resets, billing questions, and feature how-tos, leaving complex technical issues under-resourced. Average response time was 8 hours, causing customer frustration and churn.

03Solution

A conversational AI assistant was trained on the product knowledge base, historical support tickets, and documentation. The assistant handled tier-1 queries end-to-end, escalating to human agents with a structured summary when it encountered queries outside its confidence threshold. Agent-assist features provided real-time suggestions during human-handled conversations.

04Results

71%

Tier-1 ticket deflection rate

4 min

Avg. response time (down from 8 hours)

4.4/5

AI-handled CSAT score

Human agents now focus exclusively on complex technical issues, improving resolution quality and job satisfaction. The AI assistant handles the majority of support volume at a fraction of the cost of human agents.
