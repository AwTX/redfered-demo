Case Study

Ads Fraud Detection System

01Overview

An ad-tech platform was experiencing significant invalid traffic affecting campaign performance metrics and eroding advertiser trust. The rule-based detection system was reactive, catching fraud patterns only after significant spend had been wasted.

02Problem

Sophisticated bot networks mimicked human browsing behavior, evading rule-based filters. Advertisers were reporting discrepancies between platform metrics and third-party measurement tools, leading to disputes and churn.

03Solution

A multi-layered anomaly detection system was built, combining device fingerprinting, behavioral biometrics, and graph-based network analysis to identify coordinated invalid traffic patterns. Real-time scoring allowed suspicious traffic to be filtered before impressions were served, with human review queues for edge cases.

04Results

94%

Invalid traffic detected

$18M

Advertiser spend protected annually

40%

Dispute rate reduction

Advertiser confidence in platform metrics was restored, reversing a trend of measurement-related churn. The system's continuous learning loop means it adapts to new fraud patterns as they emerge.
