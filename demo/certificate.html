Case Study: Improving Website Performance

How simple optimizations increased load speed by 50%

Overview

This case study examines how a website improved its performance through lightweight optimizations. The focus was on reducing load times and enhancing user experience without major architectural changes.

Problem

The website suffered from slow load times due to large images, unused scripts, and inefficient asset delivery. Users frequently abandoned pages before they fully loaded.

Solution

Several improvements were implemented, including image compression, lazy loading, and removal of unused JavaScript. These changes reduced the overall page weight and improved rendering speed.

Results

After optimization, page load times improved by 50%. User engagement increased, bounce rates decreased, and overall user satisfaction improved significantly.
